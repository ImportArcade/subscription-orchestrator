# Epic E010 — Compliance, Security & Legal

Goal: Ensure designs honor terms of service constraints, safe handling of credentials, and privacy rules.

Epic ID: E010

Associated User Stories:
- S029 — Privacy & data retention policy (docs/gh-issues/story_S029_privacy_and_retention.md)
- S030 — Security review checklist (docs/gh-issues/story_S030_security_checklist.md)
- S031 — Legal/ToS automation gating (docs/gh-issues/story_S031_legal_automation_gating.md)

Description:
Document and implement governance around data retention, deletion/export workflows, required consent for automation, and a security checklist covering encryption and dependency scanning.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Privacy policy and retention rules documented.
- Security checklist and threat model documented.
- Consent records stored and linked to automation actions.

Priority: High
Estimate: 2.5d (sum of stories)
